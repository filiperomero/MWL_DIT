Instructions - Task 1

You are going to have access to dataset displaying the population growth for the past decades for most of territories in the euro zone (source: World DataBank ).

The data will be displayed through a map were circles will be centered in the middle of each territory. Each circle’s size is proportional to the respective territory’s Population Growth %. A green circle is used for Population Growth % positive values and a yellow circle is used for Population Growth % negative values. If a territory doesn't have a circle for a specific year it means that the Population Growth % was near 0%. After a brief animation showing the Population Growth % variation through the past years, you will be provided, at the top of the page, with a dropdown list () that will allow you to choose the year you want to explore. After selecting a year the circles in the map will be updated according with the data of the selected year.
